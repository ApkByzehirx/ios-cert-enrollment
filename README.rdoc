= ios-cert-enrollment

Sign and distribute iOS Enrollment certificates using your server's SSL Certificate

    IOSCertEnrollment.configure do |config|
        config.ssl_certificate_path = "PATH TO YOUR CERTIFICATE"
        config.ssl_key = "PATH TO YOUR CERTIFICATE KEY"
    end
